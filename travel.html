Telangana historical places

Telangana is home to various historical places, temples, beautiful cities, and lakes. It is a well-known tourist destination and boasts travel connectivity with various states. Telangana is geographically rich in minerals, especially coal, historically, it is supposed to be almost two thousand years old with links to great empires like Satvahanas, Kakatiyas, Ikshvakus, Qutb Shahis, and Asif Jahis, and spiritually, one of the first five disciples of Buddha is supposed to be a native of Telangana, although there is no exact information regarding this.
